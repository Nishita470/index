A paragraph is a series of related sentences developing a central idea, called the topic. Try to think about paragraphs in terms of thematic unity: a paragraph is a sentence or a Paragraphs add one idea at a time to your broader argument. In order to write a good paragraph, students need to understand the four essential elements of paragraph writing and how each element contributes to the whole. The four elements essential to good paragraph writing are: unity, order, coherence, and completeness. At Time4Writing, a certified teacher acts as an online writing tutor to help students build writing skills by focusing on the fundamentals.
